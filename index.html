Models

The iPad Air is available in 11-inch and 13-inch models, featuring stunning finishes in Blue, Purple, Starlight, and Space Gray. Both models offer storage capacities ranging from 128GB up to 1TB, giving users the flexibility to choose the perfect balance of style and performance.

Liquid Retina Display

The iPad Air’s Liquid Retina display delivers stunning visuals with True Tone, wide color (P3), and an antireflective coating. The 11-inch and 13-inch models offer up to 600 nits brightness and support both Apple Pencil Pro and Apple Pencil (USB-C) for precise, responsive interaction.

Apple M3 Chip

Powered by the Apple M3 chip, the iPad Air delivers exceptional performance with an 8-core CPU, 9-core GPU, and 16-core Neural Engine, supporting 8K video and ProRes for smooth, efficient multitasking and media editing.

Camera

The iPad Air’s 12MP Wide camera delivers sharp, vibrant photos with Smart HDR 4, 5x digital zoom, and auto image stabilization.
